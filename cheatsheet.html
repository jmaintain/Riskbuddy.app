Risk per Contract (Points)
Risk $ = Stop (pts) × Point Value
e.g. ES 10-point stop → 10 × $50 = $500
Risk per Contract (Ticks)
Risk $ = Stop (ticks) × Tick Value
e.g. ES 40-tick stop → 40 × $12.50 = $500
Max Contracts
Contracts = ⌊ Risk Budget ÷ Risk per Contract ⌋
Always round down — never round up
Convert Points → Ticks
Ticks = Points × (Point Value ÷ Tick Value)
e.g. ES: 1 pt × ($50 ÷ $12.50) = 4 ticks/point
